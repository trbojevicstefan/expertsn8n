About n8n Lab

Core capabilities

  • End-to-end AI workflow automation for operations, sales, support, and internal teams
  • System integrations that connect the tools your team already uses
  • Legacy migration and process redesign for teams moving away from brittle manual work

  • Workflow optimization to make automation faster, cleaner, and easier to maintain
  • Server setup and ongoing support so the automation stack stays stable as you grow

AI and AI agents
